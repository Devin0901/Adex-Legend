LEGEND NAME : CLIN

LEGEND ROLE : TANK

Clin is a sheriff in a small western town. He is known as a guardian because of his shooting skills. One day, a group of bandits invaded his town. Since he couldn't fight them all, he took the survivors away from the city. That day, Clin lost everything that was precious to him. After that, he vowed to reclaim his city. However, he needed money to buy bullets and a gun. That's why he took part in this match.
